1. Data Collection

1.1 Information We Collect

This Application may collect the following types of information:

1.1.1 Information You Provide

  • Client Data: Client names, dates of birth, contact information, etc.
  • Asset Information: Investment portfolios, stocks, bonds, deposits, insurance policies, and other financial data
  • Policy Information: Insurance companies, policy numbers, coverage amounts, beneficiaries, etc.
  • Photos and Documents: Policy photos, spreadsheet documents, etc. that you upload

1.1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Application (feature usage frequency, error logs, etc.)
  • Subscription Information: Subscription status, trial period start/end dates, payment records

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Authentication Information: We do not collect your Apple ID or password
  • Payment Information: All payment processing is handled by Apple; we do not store your credit card information
  • Location Information: We do not track your geographic location
  • Third-Party Accounts: We do not require linking to social media or other third-party accounts

2. How We Use Your Data

2.1 Primary Purposes

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: Manage your client and asset data
  • Data Synchronization: Sync data across your devices via iCloud
  • Feature Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve application features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and technical support requests
  • Subscription Management: Manage your subscription status and trial period

2.2 Data Processing

  • All data processing occurs on your device or within your iCloud account
  • We do not transfer your data to our own servers
  • OCR text recognition uses Apple's Vision framework and processes locally on your device

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Locations

  • Local Storage: Data is stored on your device using Core Data
  • Cloud Sync: Stored in your iCloud account via Apple CloudKit
  • Encryption: All data is protected by iOS and iCloud encryption

3.2 Security Measures

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Use Apple's secure frameworks (Core Data, CloudKit)
  • Data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Store data only in your iCloud account
  • Do not transfer data to third-party servers
  • Regular application updates to patch security vulnerabilities

3.3 Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device and iCloud account until you choose to delete it
  • Local data will be removed after uninstalling the application
  • iCloud data retention follows Apple's iCloud policies
  • You can delete any data at any time within the application

4. Data Sharing

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Personal Data

We will NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • Your Consent: With your explicit consent
  • Apple Services: Using services provided by Apple (iCloud, App Store)

4.2 Third-Party Services

This Application uses the following services:

Apple Services

  • iCloud / CloudKit: For cross-device data synchronization
  • StoreKit: For handling subscription payments
  • Vision Framework: For OCR text recognition (processed locally on device)

These services are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

External API Services

  • Exchange Rate API: For retrieving real-time exchange rate information
    • Purpose: Provides currency conversion for multi-currency investment products
    • Data Transmitted: Only currency codes (e.g., USD, EUR, TWD), no personal information
    • Data Received: Only public exchange rate data
    • Frequency: API calls are made only when users actively request them
    • Privacy Protection: API requests do not contain any personally identifiable information
    • Data Storage: Exchange rate data is cached only in device memory and not permanently stored
  • Stock Price API: For retrieving real-time US and Taiwan stock prices
    • Purpose: Provides real-time stock market value updates
    • Data Transmitted: Only stock symbols (e.g., AAPL, 2330.TW), no personal information
    • Data Received: Only public stock price data
    • Frequency: API calls are made only when users actively click "Update Stock Price"
    • Privacy Protection: API requests do not contain any personally identifiable information

Important Notes:

  • All API calls are anonymous requests and do not transmit any personal information
  • API service providers cannot track or identify individual users
  • We do not send your investment portfolio information to any third parties
  • Exchange rates and stock prices are public market information and do not involve personal privacy
